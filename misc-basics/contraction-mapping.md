# Contraction Mapping

`Definition (from wikipedia):` 

Let $(X, d)$ be a metric space. Then a map $T: X \rightarrow X$ is a contraction mapping on $X$ if there exists $q \in [0, 1)$ such that: 

$$ d(T(x), T(y)) \leq q d(x, y) \forall x, y \in X $$
